How it works

Light Up My Life uses Apple's Metal framework to activate your display's Extended Dynamic Range (EDR) mode — the same technology used for HDR video, applied system-wide.

1

Metal Overlay

Transparent window covers your screen

2

EDR Color Space

Extended linear P3 unlocks values above 1.0

3

Multiply Blend

Boosts your content without washing it out

4

Full Brightness

Display activates XDR mode at up to 1,600 nits
